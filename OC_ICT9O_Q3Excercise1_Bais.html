HTML

HyperText Markup Language

It is "hidden code" that helps us communicate with other people in the world wide web. It affects the look of you web page. And you can add texts, images and more.

CSS

Cascading Style Sheets

It can manipulate your browser on the way it looks. For example the color of you background and even your text. And it can also manipulate the font and even add columns to your webpage.

Bootstrap

Bootstrap Version 4

It is a technique of loading a program to your computer faster. It gives you the ability to easily create a responsive website. And it even makes responsive websites for your phone or any device needed.
